(Letterhead: New York American)

October 18th, 1926.

Dear Miss Morgan,

Replying to your letter of October 6th, enclosing report from the University of California regarding the water from the Ranch, Mr. Hearst says:

"O.K. Lets clear up the water and use the right kind of pipe."

Sincerely,

s/ J Willicombe

P.S.  Please note Mr. Hearst's notation on enclosed report...J.W.
