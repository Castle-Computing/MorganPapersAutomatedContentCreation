(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

August 4, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

I enclose a letter from Mrs. Cecil B. de Mille, thanking Mr. Hearst for a gift of china and glass to the Hollywood Studio Club.

Mr. Hearst would like to know just what this refers to.

Sincerely,
\s H. O. Hunter
