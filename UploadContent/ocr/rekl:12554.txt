SAN LUIS OBISPO CAL 1922
DEC 27TH
MR WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
137 RIVERSIDE DRIVE NEW YORK N.Y.

LETTER REGARDING FENCE RECEIVED.  HAVE MAILED YOU TODAY MAP SHOWING HOUSES, FENCES AND NATURAL GROWTH ON HILLSIDE AS NOW EXISTING.  MEN TENDER TREES AND PLANS ARE BURLAPED FOR WINTER.  PROBABLY ALL DOING INCLUDING GARDENER UNTIL AFTER NEW YEARS.  WHICH IS JUST AS WELL AS WEATHER IS STORMY AGAIN.
