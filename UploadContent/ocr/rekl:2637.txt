HEARSTCAMP SANSIMEON CALIF 330P OCT 1 1920
MISS JULIA MORGAN
MER EX BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

IN MAKING PLANS FOR TERRACE ALTERATIONS OF A PLEASE MAKE EVERY STEP TWO INCHES LOWER BEGINNING AT VERY TOP THAT IS AT THE UPPER BALCONY THEN EIGHTEEN STEPS WILL BRING US DOWN THREE FEET AT ENTRANCE TO LOGGIA SIX MORE NEW STEPS WILL TAKE US DOWN FOUR FEET MORE WHICH SHOULD BE LOW ENOUGH FOR NEW TERRACE AS LONG AS WE MAKING CHANGE MIGHT AS WELL MAKE IT COMPLETE DONT THINK WE SHOULD MAKE NEW TERRACE ANY LOWER THAN NECESSARY

HEARST
