Office of William Randolph Hearst, La Cuesta Encantada, San Simeon, California

June 29, 1935.

Dear Miss Morgan:

I particularly requested that there should not be any varnish or any stain of any 
kind put on these parchments shades.  I am sending this one back as it has been 
ruined.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst

P-S  The shade has been returned via Express by Mr. Williams
