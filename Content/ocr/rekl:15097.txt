Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher)

January 26, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

Please have some very nice fire screens made.

The one in my bedroom is good enough, but the screens generally throughout the houses are not very good.

Sincerely yours,

s/ WR Hearst
