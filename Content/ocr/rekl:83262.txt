DAY LETTER
PREPAID DEC 4 1929
Julia Morgan, Merchants Exchange Bdg., San Francisco, Calif.
Chief says all right stop work entirely on San Simeon warehouse but carry down Pancho's house to completion.
2. Thank you very much for those pictures which are greatly appreciated.
3. Hope you will have merriest Christmas and Happiest New Year of your life.
J. Willicombe.
