1925 OCT 15 AM 5 59

R LOSANGELES CALIF 14

JULIA MORGAN
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE SANFRANCISCO CALIF

I SEE NO OBJECTION TO MR. MAYBECK'S PLANS FOR DECORATING GYMNASIUM 2 I WILL BE IN LOS ANGELES TOMORROW THURSDAY AND FRIDAY SUGGEST MR JOY COME DOWN THURSDAY NIGHT AND SEE ME FRIDAY SATURDAY AM GOING WITH MRS HEARST TO RANCH WILL ARRIVE SATURDAY NIGHT HOPE YOU CAN COME TO RANCH SUNDAY WANT SEE YOU AS AM GOING EAST WEDNESDAY

WR HEARST
