APRIL 3RD 1922

MR WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
137 RIVERSIDE DRIVE NEW YORK. N.Y.  AM HAVING PRINTS MADE FOR YOU OF ALL  THE LOS ANGELES EXAMINER PROPOSITIONS, AND WILL HAVE THE PRELIMINARY SAN FRANCISCO STUDYS READY FOR THURSDAY WHEN  MR. PANCOAST LEAVES.  BOTH HAVE INTERESTING AND UNUSUAL CONDITIONS NOT EXPLAINABLE BRIEFLY.
WILL HOLD DOWN FENCE.  PALMS ALIVE AND WILL LEAF OUT AGAIN.  AM GETTING OUT CONTRACT DRAWINGS FOR GARAGE AND KITCHEN.  WANTED YOU TO SEE PLAN WITHOUT OAK AND WITH PLAZA CONNECTED TO SEE IF YOU MIGHT CONSIDER IT ADVISABLE TO MOVE MAIN BLDG BACK SOMEWHAT.
