September 7, 1929

Dear Mr. Hearst:

This is just to say that we have sent this week as you requested: (1) to Mr. Hinman 
(Paris) a sketch of the whole north terrace project, with prints of the layout, 
dimensions, etc. and notes as to location of the layout, dimensions, etc. and notes as
 to location of groups; (2) to Miss Head to be forwarded to Sir Charles Allom, full 
 plans, specifications, details, etc. of the Wyntoon pool, with green tile sample and
  full descriptions of operation.

Yours truly
