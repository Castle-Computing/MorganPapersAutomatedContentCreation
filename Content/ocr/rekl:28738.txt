San Francisco Nov. 12 1927
Mr. William Randolph Hearst
137 Riverside Drive New York City
WAS AT HILL YESTERDAY HAD A REAL STORM AND ABOUT THREE INCHES RAIN DURING WEEK BUT ROADS IN GOOD SHAPE AND NO DAMAGE COST CAGED ANIMALS NOT COUNTING HOUSING HAS AVERAGED THREE HUNDRED DOLLARS PER MONTH AND ANIMALS IN FIELDS THREE HUNDRED EIGHT DOLLARS PER MONTH THIS INCLUDING FOOD PERSONNEL VETERINARY ETC IT WILL BE A REAL HELP IF WE MAY KEEP THE ORIGINAL ALLOWANCE AND APPLY THE FUNDS ON PRESSING BACK ACCOUNTS
Julia Morgan
Charge Julia Morgan, Merchants Exchange
