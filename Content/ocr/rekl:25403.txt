JX NEWYORK NY MAY 26 1927

JULIA MORGAN
M EX BLDG SANFRAN CALIF

AM GETTING STAINED GLASS FOR WINDOWS IN REFECTORY AND WOULD LIKE TO KNOW WIDTH AND HEIGHT OF GLASS WINDOWS IN WHICH THEY COULD GO ALSO WHETHER THE ROW OF WINDOWS CENTERS ON A WINDOW OR ON A SPACE BETWEEN WINDOWS SO THAT I WILL UNDERSTAND IF I AM TO GET GLASS WINDOWS IN PAIR OR GET A CENTER WINDOW ALSO

W R HEARST
