(Letterhead: New York American)

December 15, 1920.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Referring to the enclosed communication and receipted bill amounting to $298 from the CITY AND KENTIA NURSERIES, Mr. Hearst to-day instructs me:

	" Tell Miss Morgan I have bought and paid for these plants and they should be transported to the ranch at the proper time for planting."

Sincerely, 
s/ J. Willicombe
Secretary to Mr. Hearst
