SANSIMEON CALIF FEB 14 1927

MISS JULIA MORGAN
1135 MER EX BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

FORGOT TO DISCUSS CHIMNEYS CAN YOU SEND SOMEONE DOWN TO SUPPLY DRAWINGS FOR CHIMNEYS WE ARE CHANGING ON A HOUSE 2 WE SHOULD ALSO HAVE PLAN FOR FLUE OR FIREPLACE IN EAST LOBBY THAT FIREPLACE IS VERY FINE AND SHOULD BE PRACTICAL THINK FLUE SHOULD BE ALTERED ROSSI AND I HAVE A SUGGESTION WHICH MAY BE OF USE

W R HEARST
1052P

[handwritten note]
[illegible] Feb 15th
