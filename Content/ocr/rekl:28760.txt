DAY LETTER
Nov. 17, 1927

MISS JULIA MORGAN,
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG.,
SAN FRANCISCO, CALIF.

THE HIGHER ROOF ON MAIN BUILDING LOOKS SURPRISINGLY WELL STOP GOTHIC STUFF FINE STOP CHRIS IS TRACING MORE GOTHIC MATERIAL SENT DIRECT BY BRUMMER PARAGRAPH TWO PLEASE INSTALL TRANSFORMERS NOW THREE HOPE GRADING OF GRANDE PLACE WILL PROCEED PROMPTLY COMMA ALSO INDOOR SWIMMING POOL STOP HOPE TO BE HOME BY CHRISTMAS STOP BYNE FOUND FOUNTAIN.

 W. R. HEARST.
