SANSIMEON CALIF JAN 19 1928

JULIA MORGAN
MER EXG BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

NEYLAN WILL LET YOU HAVE FORTY THOUSAND DOLLARS PER MONTH AND I WILL LET YOU HAVE EXTRA FIVE OR TEN THOUSAND IF NECESSARY STOP RICHARD CLARK WILL TAKE CARE OF SLATTERYS BILLS WHICH HE TELLS ME ARE AMOUNTING AT PRESENT TO TEN THOUSAND DOLLARS PER MONTH PART OF WHICH AT LEAST USED TO BE PAID BY ROSSI STOP I THINK WITH THIS ARRANGEMENT WE WILL BE ABLE TO CARRY THE FULL FORCE

W R HEARST
