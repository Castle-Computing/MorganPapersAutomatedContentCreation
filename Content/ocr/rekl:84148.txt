December 6, 1930.
Miss Julia Morgan,
Merchants Exchange,
San Francisco, California.
Dear Miss Morgan:
Enclosed herewith is check of Hearst Consolidated Publications, Inc., payable to you in the sum of $10000.00.
This check is for the account of Mr. William Randolph Hearst. 
Would you please acknowledge receipt of same to this office?
Very sincerely yours,

[handwritten]
File
John Francis Neylan
