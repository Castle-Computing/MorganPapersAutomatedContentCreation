Office of William Randolph Hearst, La Cuesta Encantada, San Simeon, California

March 7, 1935.

MEMORANDUM FOR MR. LOORZ--
FROM W.R. HEARST.

I am informed that direct current is desirable for quick and sparking operation of 
the tower bells.

Alternating current, if we have it, can be transformed into direct current by 
transformers.
