(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)


Sept. 27, 1926.

Dear Miss Morgan:

"I do not ring for the souls of the dead but for the ears of the living"

is a good inscription; but perhaps we can find something in modern American poetry that would be more inspiring than a Latin quotation.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst
