MAY 14, 1926.

MR. WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST,
AMBASSADOR HOTEL
LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA.

VERMONT MARBLE MAN HERE SAYS PLANTS ALL WORKING ON VERDI ANTIQUE NO BROCATELLO IN FIRST SHIPMENT NOTICED MONDAY AMBASSADOR POOL LOOKS VERY SHALLOW WOULD NOT GREEN SIDES WITH THE FLICKER OF WHITE MARKINGS MAKE FOR RICHNESS AND DEPTH WILL MAIL TODAY THE PHOTOGRAPHS OF ALL MANTELS HERE OR SANSIMEON.

JULIA MORGAN

CHG JULIA MORGAN
