1926 APR 28 PM 9 25

JX NEWYORK NY 28

MISS JULIA MORGAN
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

FOR RANCH I WANT VERDE ANTIQUE ENTIRELY FOR THE BIG OBLONG POOL YOU MIGHT WANT TO USE BROCADILLO FOR THE SMALLER SQUARE POOL AND THE WALLS SUPPORTING IT KINDLY WIRE YOUR OPINION TWO AT THE UNIVERSITY I WOULD USE VERDE ANTIQUE UNLESS COLOR SCHEME OF THE BUILDING DEMAND OTHER COLORS IN WHICH CASE PLEASE WIRE SUGGESTION THREE I WOULD NOT USE ANY MARBLE WHICH HAD ANY DEFECTS LIKE EFFLORESCENSE USE ONLY THE BEST AND MOST DURABLE

W R HEARST.
