(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

June 2, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

Please note the attached letter from Mr. Byne, which Mr. Hearst requested be referred to you.

He said, "would certainly buy the chimney piece.  Are there any other good things?"

Sincerely yours,

s/ J Willicombe

(Encl.)
