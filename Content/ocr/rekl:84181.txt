Office of William Randolph Hearst, La Cuesta Encantada, San Simeon, California

[Handwritten:  Cabled Dec 26th]

Dec. 22, 1930.

Dear Miss Morgan:

I should have statement from Byne.  Have paid for ALCANTARA which apparently is 
unobtainable.  This should defray all other bills and leave surplus.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst

Miss Julia Morgan,
San Francisco, Calif.
