1928 JAN 16 PM 1 46

F77 32.SANSIMEON CALIF 16 100P

MISS JULIA MORGAN.
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE SANFRANCISCO CALIF.

PLEASE ERECT HIGH AND CAPACIOUS WATER TOWER AND TANK FOR THE WAREHOUSES AT SANSIMEON AND PUT IN NECESSARY HYDRANTS ETCETERA FOR PERFECT PROTECTION PLEASE ALSO SEGREGATE MATERIAL STORED IN MANNER WE DISCUSSED.

W R HEARST



[Handwritten note at bottom of above Telegram]

Discussed Jan 22nd at SS.  Agreed to use version at ranch hill & to build concrete warehouse
