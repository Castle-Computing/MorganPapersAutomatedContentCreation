OCTOBER 8TH 1927

MR. WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
LOS ANGELES EXAMINER,
LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA.

ON RECEIPT YOUR FIRST MESSAGE HAD SENT WORK TO FARM APPLICANT TO GO LOS ANGELES SEE YOU PERSONALLY I COULD NOT REACH HIM TONIGHT SO PROBABLY HE IS ON WAY HE AGREED TO SALARY YOU SUGGESTED THREE HUNDRED PLUS SUITABLE HOUSING AT SANSIMEON IF YOU TAKE HIM WOULD MUCH LIKE TO TALK OVER RELATIONSHIPS INVOLVED WITH YOU BEFORE HIS INDUCTION IF YOU WOULD CARE TO WILL BE WORKING ON HILL MONDAY AND HAVE TO BE HERE TUESDAY BUT OTHERWISE WILL HOLD THE WEEK FOR YOU

JULIA MORGAN
