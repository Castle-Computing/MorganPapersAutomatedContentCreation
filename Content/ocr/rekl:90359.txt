September 29, 1937.

Mr. Edward G. Woods,
959 - 8th Avenue, 
New York, N.Y.

Dear Mr. Woods:

	Replying to your letter of the 21st,
regarding built-in antiques in Mr. Hearst's home, 
I will ask Miss Moran immediately to prepare
the list you request, and send copy of it to you.

	Yours very truly, 
	
	William W. Murray
