(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

October 5, 1927.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Referring to the recent gift of clothing to the Florence Crittendon Home, Mr. Hearst directed me to ask you if there is any more stuff which we will never use and on which we are paying storage.

Sincerely,

s/ H.O. Hunter
Secretary to Mr. Hearst
