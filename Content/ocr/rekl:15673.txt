1926 MAY 27 PM 7 16

LOSANGELES CALIF 27

JULIA MORGAN
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

I SUPPOSE THAT WE ARE HEATING POOLS AT UNIVERSITY WE SHOULD ALSO USE ELECTRIC HEATING AT RANCH HEATING WILL BE VERY NECESSARY ON AUTUMN AND EARLY SPRING DAYS WE SHOULD HEAT TO SEVENTY DEGREES

W R HEARST.
