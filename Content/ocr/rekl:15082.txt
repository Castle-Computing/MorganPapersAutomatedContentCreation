(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher)

January 12, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

I think the cost of the marble for the patio vestibule and loggia above is not so very bad and probably we better have marble.  That is, probably only $4,000 for the vestibule and $4,000 for the loggia.

Sincerely yours,

s/ W.R. Hearst
