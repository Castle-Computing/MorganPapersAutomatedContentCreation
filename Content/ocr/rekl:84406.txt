John Francis Neylan
Attorney at Law
Crocker First National Bank Building
San Francisco

July 28, 1931

Miss Julia Morgan,
Merchants Exchange, 
San Francisco, Calif.

Dear Miss Morgan:
	
	Pursuant to request by telephone from
Mr. Willicombe today, I hand you herewith check in
the amount of $10,000.00, payable to your order.
	will you please acknowledge receipt on 
copy of this letter, and oblige, 
	Yours very truly,
	
	Secretary to Mr. Neylan
