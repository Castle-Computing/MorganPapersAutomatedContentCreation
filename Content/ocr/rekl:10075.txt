APRIL 15TH 1922
MR WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
137 RIVERSIDE DRIVE NEW YORK. N.Y. 

SECURED YOUR NILES SEQUOIA TAKES 27OO RED AND BLACK TILES AS PER SAMPLE YOU APPROVED FOR MAIN TERRACE FILLERS ORIGINAL EASTERN TILE COST 3.50 FT.  JUST SUCCEEDED IN GETTING GOOD SAN JOSE REPRODUCTION BUT STILL EXPENSIVE AT 2.00 PER FT. AGAINST RED TILE AT 50 CENTS WOULD NOT GAY CENTER SPOT IN EACH LARGE SQUARE DO.  YOUR NILE TREES WENT DOWN THIS BOAT WILL TAKE ALL HANDS AT LEAST WEEK TO GET THESE INTO HIGH CANYONS BUT KEEP SAYS NOW IS TIME.
