Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., 
Los Angeles

April 10, 1930.

Dear Miss Morgan:

I would like to use some colored fixtures in some of the new bathrooms that we will be 
creating at San Simeon.

I think the idea of baths and wash stands in color pottery is a pleasing variation.

Sincerely yours,

s/ WR Hearst
