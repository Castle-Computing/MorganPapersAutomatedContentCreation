1926 JAN 11 AM 10 43

R LOSANGELES CALIF 11 935 A

JULIA MORGAN
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

MR PARKS ANXIOUS TO HAVE WORK ON CHICKEN HOUSES AND ALL SUCH WORK AROUND FARM HOUSE DONE UNDER HIS SUPERVISION HE THINKS HE CAN SAVE MONEY AND ALSO PREVENT HIS OWN MEN FROM BECOMING DISSATISFIED WILL YOU KINDLY ARRANGE THIS I THINK PRESENT SUPERINTENDENT NOT SUITABLE AND IS UNNECESSARY EXPENSE AND UNNECESSARILY EXPENSIVE ADVISE REMOVING HIM AND GIVING PARKS CHANCE

WR HEARST.
