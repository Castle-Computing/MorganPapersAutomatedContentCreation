STRAIGHT MESSAGE RUSH PREPAID DEC 13 1929
Julia Morgan, Merchants Exchange Bdg., SAN FRANCISCO, CALIF.
I think best break camp this S_aturday but we can always assemble enough men any time later when weather is fine to finish pouring first floor amusement wing. Am afraid moving talkie outfit to attic would involve considerable expense. Perhaps better wait until regular theatre covered in.
W R HEARST.
