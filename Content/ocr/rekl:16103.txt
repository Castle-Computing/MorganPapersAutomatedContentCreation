(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)


October 2, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

I return herewith plans for the propose kitchen wing.  Mr. Hearst said:

"Return this to Miss Morgan. Tell her I think it is very nice indeed and to please construct it as indicated."

Sincerely yours,

s/ H. O. Hunter
