(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

February 14, 1927.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Enclosed please find Mr. Hearst's check for $8,500-- in response to your telegram-- same being first payment on the bells for hill top, and he asks if you will kindly hurry the bells along.

Sincerely,

s/ J Willicombe

Miss Julia Morgan,

Merchants Exchange Blg.,
San Francisco, Calif.
