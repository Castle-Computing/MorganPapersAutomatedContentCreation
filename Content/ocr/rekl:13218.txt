December 1, 1923

Mr. William Randolph Hearst,
137 Riverside Drive,
New York, N.Y.

Dear Mr. Hearst:

The enclosed from Mr. Byne will probably have to come back here, but will show what he has done.  If there is any mistake and you do not want the material it can be readily disposed of here.  Mr. Byne thought you wanted it.

Yours very truly,

JM-deM

Enclosed Mr. Byne's letter of Nov. 12, 1923, consular invoice, bills for packing and boxing of doors & shutters, 60 p bill for packing and boxing ceiling, 1,000 pesetas, bill for doors and shutters 1,600,  receipt for owner of ceiling 9,500, Statement of Arthur Byne 13,966
