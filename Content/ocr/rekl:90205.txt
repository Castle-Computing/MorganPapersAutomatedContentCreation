February 3, 1937

Mr. John D. Costello
Crocker 1st National Bank Bldg.
San Francisco, California 

Dear Mr. Costello:

	Thank you very much for your 
letter of the 1st, together with a copy of 
the Decision of the Unites States Customs
Court in connection with the furniture from
the interior of a 15th Century Aragonese
Castle. 

	We are returning to you your 
copy of the Decision, also one of our copies. 

	Very truly yours, 
	Julia Morgan 
	
	JM:F   By
	Enc.
