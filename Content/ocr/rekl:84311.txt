Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles

April 11, 1931.

Dear Miss Morgan:

I enclose photographs of the Cassou statues.  These things are arriving.  They 
complete the statuary for the pool fountain.

Don't you think we should now proceed to erect this statuary and proceed to 
complete the pool.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst
