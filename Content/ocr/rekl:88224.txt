John Francis Neylan
Attorney at Law
Crocker First National Bank Building
San Francisco

April 20, 1936

Miss Julia Morgan,
Merchants Exchange Bldg.,
San Francisco, Calif.

Dear Miss Morgan:

	Mr. Willicombe asks me to refer to you for
approval the enclosed bills of Stringer Storage Company.
He asks us to pay the account as approved by you. 
Will you, therefore, return them to me at your convenience?

	Thanking you for your attention, I am, 
		Very truly yours,
		A. M. Flood
		Secretary to Mr. Neylan
