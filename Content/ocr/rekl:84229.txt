American Express Company

January 23, 1931

Attention, Mr. Le Feaver

Dear Mr. LeFeaver:-

Further to our letter of May 3, 1930 re twenty five (25) cases Lamps which 
arrived on the steamer "Feltre" consigned to you by F. Romanelli, Florence, Italy.

We now attach Notice of Hearing from the United States Customs Court showing that 
this case will be heard at a term of the Court to be held in the Office of United 
States Appraiser in San Francisco on March 17, 1931.

If there is anything further that we can do to assist you, please advise and oblige.

Yours truly,

s/F L Reighton
FOREIGN FREIGHT AGENT.
