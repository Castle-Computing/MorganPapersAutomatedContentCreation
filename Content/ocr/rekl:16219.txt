(Letterhead: New York American)

January 19, 1927.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Here are a number of things that I found in a book on the World's Fair, and some of which we may want to use at San Simeon.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst

----------------

COURT OF ABUNDANCE-- Topmost group under arch of tower-- female figure seated on throne.

FOUNTAIN IN FRONT OF FESTIVAL HALL AND IN FESTIVAL POOL.

RISING AND SETTING SUN FOUNTAIN in Court of Universe.

NYMPH WITH HEAD OF ORPHEUS-- Panel from Fine Arts Rotunda.

FOUNTAIN OF BEAUTY and the BEAST COURT OF FLOWERS.
