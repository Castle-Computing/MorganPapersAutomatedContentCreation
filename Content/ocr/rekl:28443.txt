1927 JUN 2 PM 7 19

JULIA MORGAN.
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF.

I SUGGEST PLANTING PALMS AND YEWS IN EXTRA LARGE BOXES SO THAT THE BOXES CAN BE MOVED WITHOUT INJURY TO PLANTS IT IS NECESSARY FOR ME TO BE ON THE GROUND TO LOCATE PLANTS INTELLIGENTLY THEREFORE PLEASE PUT THEM WHERE YOU THINK THEY SHOULD BE BUT IN SUCH MANNER THAT THEY CAN BE MOVED.

W R HEARST.
