DEC 14TH 1921

WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
RIVERSIDE DRIVE, NEW YORK, N.Y.

HAVE SENT WOOD TO CLOSE CAMP ON TWENTY THIRD VISITED OF THIRTY FIRST AS PRACTICALLY ALL MEN GO AWAY FOR CHRISTMAS TO NEW YEAR HOLIDAY EXCEPT LOCAL PEOPLE.  BUT TO LEAVE THE TWENTY SEVENTH, ALL OTHERS TO STAY AWAY  UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE.  WILL BE GLAD OF THE OPPORTUNITY TO START AFRESH.

JULIA MORGAN
