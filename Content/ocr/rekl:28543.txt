ONE MAIN TROUBLE MISS WORN DOESNT COME REGULARLY OR OFTEN ENOUGH TRY TO ARRANGE SUNDAY AND MONDAY EVERY OTHER WEEK COMPENSATION ACCORDINGLY STOP I STIPULATE TWO DAYS AT TIME TO ALLOW CAREFUL STUDY OF CONDITIONS AND EVERY TWO WEEKS ON STATED DAYS TO KEEP IN CLOSE TOUCH WITH GARDENER AND WITH MYSELF STOP I NEVER SEE HER ANY TO PREVENT CONFUSION AND CONTRADICTION IS IMPORTANT TO SEE ME OCCASSIONALLY
