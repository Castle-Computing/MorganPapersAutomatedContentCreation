WASHINGTON DC DEC 26 1921
JULIA MORGAN
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO

THINK WASHBURN HAS MANY GOOD QUALITIES AND BELIEVE  WILL BE SATISFACTORY WITH ADDITIONAL FOREMEN AND OFFICE HELP YOU HAVE PROVIDED TROUBLE FORMERLY WAS FORCE NOT DRIVEN WASHBURN NOT BEING DRIVER DOUBTLESS YOU HAVE PROVIDED MEN WHO ARE TWO MAKE DOORS FOR LOWER LOBBY FOR HOUSE A.  AS YOU SUGGEST THREE UNTREATED REDWOOD SUFFICIENTLY SATISFACTORY HAVE YOU ORDERED REDWOOD POSTS YET WHAT IS YOUR FINAL OPINION ABOUT RELATIVE MERITS REDWOOD AND CONCRETE POSTS.

W R HURST
