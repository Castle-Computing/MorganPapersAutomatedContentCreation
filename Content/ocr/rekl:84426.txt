(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

June 7, 1928.

Dear Miss Morgan:

I enclose picture of two Venitian windows.  I cannot use these at Sands Point, but we might possibly have use for them at the ranch.

Will you kindly let me know.

Sincerely,

s/ W.R. Hearst
