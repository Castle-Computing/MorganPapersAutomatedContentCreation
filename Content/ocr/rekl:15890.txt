June 19, 1926.

Dear Mr. Hearst:

This is lovely, -- thank you for showing it to me.  I hope Mr. Beach will think better of the price.

Yours very truly,


[note at the bottom of the letter]
Returned with this letter a photograph of "Rising Mists of the Sea" by Chester Beach and Mr. Beach's letter to Mr. Hearst dated May 18th, 1926
