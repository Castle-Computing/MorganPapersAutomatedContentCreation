Office of William Randolph Hearst, La Cuesta Encantada, San Simeon, California

Oct. 4, 1929.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Mr. Hearst asks if you will send him a list of modern furniture that is in storage in 
San Francisco.  He says he understands there is some there.  Thanking you,

Sincerely yours,

s/ J Willicombe

Miss Julia Morgan,
San Francisco, Calif.
