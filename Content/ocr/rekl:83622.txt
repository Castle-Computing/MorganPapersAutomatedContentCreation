John Francis Neylan
Attorney at Law
Crocker First National Bank Building
San Francisco

May 9, 1930

Miss Julia Morgan,
Merchants Exchange Building,
San Francisco.

Dear Miss Morgan:
	
	I have yours of May 8th, concerning customs duty paid under protest on carved 
marble lamps.

	Will you let me have a record of the customs lot number and also the detailed bills.
	
	I will then look into it.
	
	Very sincerely yours,
	
	John Francis Neylan 
	
	May 10 1930 Rec'd
