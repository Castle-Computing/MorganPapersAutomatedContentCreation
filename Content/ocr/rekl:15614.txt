May 20, 1926

Dear Mr. Hearst:

As per your request of May 18th, we are forwarding you, care of the Los Angeles Examiner, via the Los Angeles Steamship Company, the two mantels, packed as follows:

	Cartes #1139, 1140, 1141, 1142, containing mantel #F 4, received here in the 	12th car from New York.

	Crates #2604, 2605, 2606, 2607, containing mantel #507, received here in the 	34th car from New York.

Bills of lading for this shipment will be forwarded to Mr. Hunter of the Los Angeles Examiner.

Very truly yours,

Julia Morgan
