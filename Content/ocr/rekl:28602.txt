SANSIMEON CALIF SEP 16 1927

JULIA MORGAN
1135 MER EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF

HAS STAINED GLASS FOR REFECTORY FROM DEMOTTES REACHED COAST STOP WOULD LIKE TO HAVE IT PUT UP WHILE I AM HERE STOP PLEASE ANSWER CARE LOSANGELES EXAMINER TWO IF POSSIBLE PLEASE CANCEL REQUISITION FIFTY TWO THIRTY SIX FOR FENCING AS WE WANT CYCLONE FENCING WITH TWO INCH MESH STOP THE PRESENT MESH IS TOO LARGE AND FOXES GET THROUGH STOP WE WANT TO KEEP OUT ALL VARMINTS

WR HEARST
803P
