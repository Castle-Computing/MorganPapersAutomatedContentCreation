1926 OCT 25 AM 12 48

MISS JULIA MORGAN.

MERCHANTS EXCHANGE BLDG SANFRANCISCO CALIF.

PLEASE HURRY ASSEMBLY ROOM AND LIBRARY TO COMPLETION SEVERAL NOTABLES PARTIES INTEND VISITING RANCH AND I WOULD LIKE IT PRESENTABLE AS POSSIBLE KINDLY COMPLETE AS MUCH OUTSIDE WORK AS POSSIBLE ALSO BEFORE RAINS BEGIN ASK ROSE TO SURFACE NEW WALLS FOR WINTER USE WILL RETURN IN NOVEMBER.

HEARST.
