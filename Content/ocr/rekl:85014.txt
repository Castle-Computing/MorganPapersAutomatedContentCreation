January 25, 1932

Mr. O. H. Tunell
International Studio Art Corporation
387 Southern Boulevard
New York

Dear Mr. Tunell:

We have received your letter of January 20th enclosing your shipping list #3 and 
two photographs covering 110 yards of gold damask and 182 yards of brocatelle sent 
to San Simeon by express.

Yours very truly,

Julia Morgan
