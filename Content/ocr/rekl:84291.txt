Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles

March 30, 1931.

Dear Miss Morgan:

I am going to try to buy the ceiling of the Emperors of which Mr. Byk showed us 
photographs. It comes up at Auction shortly.  I think it will be much better for 
the ceiling of the music room than the one we have, because it is about the size 
of the roof.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst
