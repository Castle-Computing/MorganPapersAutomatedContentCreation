(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

December 30, 1925

Dear Miss Morgan:

Referring to the attached.

I have no doubt that the way Mr. Humrich desires to proceed is the best way to proceed with respect to the refectory ceiling.

Will you kindly notify him to that effect, and I will forward a check.

Sincerely yours,

s/ WR Hearst

(Encl.)
