(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

October 2, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

 I am sending under separate cover Exclopedia of Practical Quotations.

I don't see why each one of the bells should not have an inscription - each one of the big bells anyhow.

What do you think of those I have marked?

Sincerely yours,

s/ W.R. Hearst
