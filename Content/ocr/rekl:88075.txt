Office of William Randolph Hearst, La Cuesta Encantada, San Simeon, California

July 9, 1935.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Many thanks for your letter of July 5th regarding the parchment shades.

The four shades in my Gothic living room (West side) have shrunk for their frames.  
Could these possibly be repaired?

Sincerely yours,

s/ WR Hearst
