(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher, Broadway at Eleventh St., Los Angeles)

February 27, 1928.

Dear Miss Morgan:

Enclosed find clipping of article about 'Smokeless Chimney Top.'

This seems to me a very sound and practical suggestion and I would like to try it out on some of our chimneys.

Sincerely,

s/ WR Hearst
