SEPTEMBER 16, 1925.

MR. WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST,
AMBASSADOR HOTEL
LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA.

WAS PLANNING TO TAKE OUR ENGINEER SEE RAPELLO AND DAM SITES SUNDAY COMING DOWN TO REPORT TO YOU MONDAY IF THIS IS SATISFACTORY TO YOU DO YOU WANT ANIMAL MAN HE IS RICHARD ADDISON SIXTY LINDA ROSA AVENUE PASADENA THINK ITALIAN CYPRESS WOULD BE FINE ROSE MAZE DIFFICULT TO KEEP LOOKING WELL ALL YEAR.

JULIA MORGAN

CHG JULIA MORGAN
