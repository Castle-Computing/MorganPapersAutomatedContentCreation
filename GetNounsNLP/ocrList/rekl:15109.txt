(Letterhead: Los Angeles Examiner, Office of the Publisher)

January 31, 1926

Dear Miss Morgan:

Please put the "marquetry set" in the Stringer warehouse.  

I do not know where we could use them at San Simeon unless at the ranch house.

If you think they can be placed there advantageously, you might send them to the ranch for that purpose.

Sincerely yours,

s/ WR Hearst



[Handwritten note on above Letter]

LEF boxed  OK JEF Bekins ordered Boxes M-126 to M-143 inclusive
