June 11, 1924.

Mr. William Randolph Hearst,
137 Riverside Drive,
New York, N.Y.

Dear Mr. Hearst:

Enclosed is a list of articles from the catalogues of the Tolentino and Canessa sales for which I can see good places.  Those marked with a blue cross wold be especially useful now.  The important cope from the Garland sale we have already received.

Yours very truly,
JM-deM
