JANUARY 12 1932

MISS JULIA MORGAN
WILL CALL AT WESTERN UNION
SOUTHERN PACIFIC STATION
LOS ANGELES CALIFORNIA

NOTHING NEW ON HOSPITAL CLIENT WOULD LIKE CERTAIN IRON GRILLES INSTEAD OF SHUTTERS AT 
BEACH HOUSE DATA MAILED TO SOKOLOW FOR YOU DONT THINK IT PRESSING MISTER HEARST AT SAN 
SIMEON AND WOULD LIKE TO HAVE YOU STOP OFF THERE IF CONVENIENT I WAS THERE MONDAY AT 
CLIENTS REQUEST TO MEET BELL MAN NO BUDGETS YET

J. H. LEFEAVER

CHARGE TO JULIA MORGAN
MERCHANTS EXCHANGE
