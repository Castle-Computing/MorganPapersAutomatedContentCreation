NOVEMBER 26, 1935

MR. WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
RITZ TOWER
NEW YORK

AT SANSIMEON YESTERDAY CHECKING UP FOUND CONCRETE OF ENLARGED NEPTUNE UPPER POOL 
NEW STAIRWAY UNDER OAK WALLS AND CHANGES BETWEEN C HOUSE AND WEST TERRACE AGREED ON 
JUST POURED THINK IT WOULD TAKE UNTIL MID DECEMBER WITH SMALL PRESENT FORCE TO LEAVE 
IN GOOD SHAPE ALL PAVEMENTS WOULD BE COMPLETED BUT PROBABLY NOT STEP FINISH

JULIA MORGAN
