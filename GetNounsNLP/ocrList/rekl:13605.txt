MAY 10, 1924

MR. WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST,
137 RIVERSIDE DRIVE,
NEW YORK
 DO YOU WANT ME TO TAKE COMPETITION BIDS AND CONTRACT FOR NEW WATER LINES OR BUY MATERIAL AND GET COMPETENT FOREMAN AND SEPARATE CREW.  I EXPECTED TO HAVE PIPE FOR LINE FROM RESERVOIR TO CAMP ON THIS WEEKS BOAT AND HAD ARRANGED FOR SEPARATE CREW READY TO BEGIN WORK UNDER CAMP MECHANIC ON THIS PART OF WORK THIS WEEK.

JULIA MORGAN
