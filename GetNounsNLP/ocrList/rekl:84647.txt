November 20, 1931

Miss Julia Morgan,
Merchants Exchange,
San Francisco, California.

Dear Miss Morgan:
	
	I enclose herewith check of William
Randolph Hearst, by John Francis Neylan, Trusteo,
in the amount of $20,000.00, which sum is being
paid you for Mr. Hearst's personal account.
	Please acknowledge receipt of check
on copy of this letter herewith end return to me.

	Very sincerely yours,
	John Francis Neylan
