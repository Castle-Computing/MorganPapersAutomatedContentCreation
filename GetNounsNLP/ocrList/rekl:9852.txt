February 21, 1922.
Mr. William Randolph Hearst,
137 Riverside Drive,
New York City, N.Y.
Dear Mr. Hearst:
Attached are two designs for B chimney caps, using frieze tile as panels. they will raise the chimneys by the height of this cap. Would you prefer to have them more simple?
Attached also is a suggestion for the chimney cap of C.
Yours very truly,
JM-deM
