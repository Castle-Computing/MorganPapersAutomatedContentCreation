BOSTON MASS 345P MAY 18 1921
MISS JULIA MORGAN
SANFRANCISCO CALIF

AM SENDING FINAL SHIPMENT WHICH CONTAINS SEVERAL FINE OLD EXTERIOR DOORS WITH OLD NAILS AND HASPS ON THEM WE SHOULD USE THEM FOR LOWER DOORS OF B AND C NO STATUARY YET CAN WE PLACE CROUCHING VENUS IN TILED NICHE BACK OF POOLE AT C OR DO YOU KNOW BETTER PLACE

W R HEARST
