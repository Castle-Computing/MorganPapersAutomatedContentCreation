March 15, 1922.
Mr. William Randolph Hearst,
137 Riverside Drive,
New York City, N.Y.
WAS DELAYED ON ENGLISH HOUSE SKETCHES. MAILED THEM THURSDAY MARCH NINETH. WAS AT CAMP YESTERDAY. STILL TOO WET TO PLANT ORCHARD BUT ALL TREES EXCEPT CITROUS ON HAND PLANTED. STILL TO COLD TO PLANT CITRUS TREES. HUMRICH HAS STARTED PREPARING UPPER CEILINGS FOR SAMPLES. 
Julia Morgan
Chg. Julia Morgan
1135 Merchants Exchange Bldg.,
San Francisco, Calif.
