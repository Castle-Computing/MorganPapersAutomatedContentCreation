Wm Randolph Hearst May 19th
282 137 Riverside Drive, New York, N.Y.
Your letter and telegram regarding water supply received. I had understood your idea. Will explain to Fairchild and his engineer - Suggest springs should be cleaned out widened and dispensed at source and ample concrete catch basins be made so that lose at these points be stopped and amount water available be at once determinable. This [illeg.] no great expense and could be ready by time pipe line is determined. 
Julia Morgan
