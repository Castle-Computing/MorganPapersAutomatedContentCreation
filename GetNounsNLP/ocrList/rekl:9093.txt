NIGHT LETTER
SANFRANCISCO
DEC. 19, 1921
MR. WILLIAM RANDOLPH HEARST
137 RIVERSIDE DRIVE,
NEW YORK, N.Y.

WILL TALK OVER RUNNING FENCE WITH LEE THURSDAY I UNDERSTAND THE GENERAL LINES YOU WISH FOLLOWED I FIND THAT ACCOUNT ELABORATE CHARACTER OF TRIM IN BOTH UPPER BEDROOMS IT IS NECESSARY TO GAIN ALL WALL SPACE POSSIBLE AT FIREPLACE ENDS WOULD YOU BE WILLING TO OMIT BOOKCASES IN ONE OR BOTH ROOMS OR TAKE (?) SHALLOW.

JM
